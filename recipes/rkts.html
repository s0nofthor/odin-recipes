Rice Krispy Treats

Description

The classic name brand version of these are a mainstay of the lunchbox. While they are almost without exception terrific, what makes them even better is how relatively easy it is to whip up and how simple it is to slip in a few ingredients that take them from a quilty pleasure to an ideal snack that will still give you nutrients you need.

Ingredients

  • 28g Butter
  • 50g 2% Milk
  • 4g Vanilla Extract
  • 1g salt
  • 192g Max Mallows
  • 97.2g Protein Powder
  • 148g Crispy Rice Cereal
  • Oil Spray

While you can use more generic versions of the linked ingredients, you will get the best result by using the ones recommended

Steps

  1. Add butter, milk, vanilla extract and salt to a pot
  2. Put the pot on a stovetop and set to medium low heat
  3. Add Crispy Rice to a large bowl
  4. Add protein powder to a medium sized bowl
  5. When butter is nearly melted, mix the pot and add the Max Mallows while setting the heat to medium
  6. Stir until the Max Mallows are almost melted, then add in the protein powder
  7. Immediately remove from heat and stir until combined, making sure to do it before it cools
  8. Add the Crispy Rice and stir carefully but quickly to combine without letting it cool
  9. Spray a square pan with oil and put the Rice Crispy Treats in
  10. Spray your hands with oil and press the treats into shape, filling the pan
  11. Once the pan is level, turn it upside down and remove the treats
  12. Cut and eat!
