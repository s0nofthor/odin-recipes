Fried Chicken

Description

This dish is one of the best out there, the only thing holding it back is that it seems impossible to find a good way to make it without using a crazy amount of frying oil. Well, the impossible seems more probable when you look at this recipe for fried chicken that only needs an air fryer to cook in. This makes it super easy to cook and much less of a hassle to clean, while also taking surprisingly little time.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 340g Chicken Breast
  • 20g Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Hot Sauce
  • 30g Cornflakes
  • 2 Hamburger Buns
  • Pickles

Sauce

  • 40g Greek Yogurt
  • 20g Honey
  • 8g Mustard
  • 25g SF BBQ Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/4 tsp Mushroom Powder

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry with a towel
  2. Beat meat on worksurface with fist to tenderize and flatten a little
  3. Cut each breast in half
  4. Prepare one plate with flour, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper
  5. Prepare a second plate with whisked eggs and hotsauce mixed together
  6. Prepare a final plate with crushed cornflakes
  7. Dip each chicken fillet in the plates following the number order and making sure they are completely covered by ingredients
  8. Once they are all breaded, cook two breasts in air fryer for 8 minutes on each side
  9. While waiting for it to cook, mix all sauce ingredients together, slice pickle, and toast buns
  10. Assemble once the air fryer is done and take a massive bite out of some super crispy and juicy chicken sandwiches!
